## Category 1: RED CARPET

### Category Description
High-glamour, statement-making looks, wearable art, designed for global premieres, galas, and fashion's biggest stages. Think cinematic elegance, Met Gala, Oscars, AMAs, fashion precision, and couture-level vision, or alternatively avant-garde, futuristic in the style of Iris Van Herpen.

### What Types of Clothing Belong Here
- **Evening gowns** (floor-length, dramatic silhouettes)
- **Cocktail dresses** (statement-making, high-fashion)
- **Tuxedos and formal suiting** (architectural, avant-garde takes)
- **Capes and dramatic outerwear** (sculptural, show-stopping)
- **Avant-garde pieces** (wearable art, futuristic elements)
- **Statement accessories** (oversized, architectural jewelry and headpieces)

### Design Considerations
- Drama and impact
- Sculptural elements
- Luxe fabrications (sequins, metallics, silk, organza, tulle)
- Red carpet presence
- Architectural innovation
- Couture-level construction details
- Show-stopping silhouettes

### Visual Background Specifications
**Setting:** Dramatic red carpet, gala entrance, or fashion runway with dramatic lighting

**Action/Motion Ideas:**
- Model walking toward camera with flowing train behind
- Fabric billowing in wind (dress, cape, or train)
- Twirling motion showing dress volume and movement
- Stepping out of luxury vehicle
- Flash photography creating light effects
- Ascending grand staircase with dramatic lighting
- Fabric catching air and lifting slightly

**Color Palette for Visual:**
- Deep blacks and rich crimsons
- Metallic gold, silver, or rose gold accents
- Dramatic lighting (spotlights, camera flashes)
- Gradient overlay: Black to Crimson for text readability

**Photography Style:**
- High-fashion editorial
- Dramatic lighting and shadows
- Cinematic quality
- Glamorous, high-contrast

## Technical Specifications for All Categories

### Image Requirements
- **Dimensions:** Minimum 1920x1080px for full-width backgrounds
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for desktop, 9:16 for mobile
- **Quality:** High resolution, editorial-grade
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB per image)

### Animation/Motion Specifications
**Recommended Motion Effects:**
- Subtle parallax scrolling (background moves slower than foreground)
- Gentle fabric movement (using cinemagraph or subtle video loops)
- Hover effects revealing more detail
- Smooth transitions between categories
- Ken Burns effect (slow zoom/pan on static images)

**Accessibility Considerations:**
- Provide reduced-motion option for users
- Never convey information through motion alone
- Ensure text overlay remains readable during motion
- Test for motion sensitivity

### Color Overlay Guidelines
Each category uses specific gradient overlays to ensure text readability:

1. **Red Carpet:** Black to Crimson (#000000 to #DD292F)
2. **Loungewear:** Warm Beige to Soft Coral (#EDDBD5 to #FF8585)
3. **Athleisurewear:** Deep Blue to Mint (#1B365D to #45FFCA)
4. **Streetwear:** Black to Coral (#000000 to #FF5050)
5. **Proleisurewear™:** Cool Grey to Deep Blue (#808080 to #1B365D)

### Interactive Elements for Each Category Block
- **Hover State:** Slightly darkens overlay, reveals additional design considerations
- **Click Action:** Expands full category description with more examples
- **CTA Button:** "Enter [Category Name] - $45" prominently displayed
- **Smooth Scroll:** Categories linked to anchor tags for easy navigation
