# Image Assets Guide for She Is AI Fashion Awards

## Directory Structure

```
public/images/
├── categories/          # Category section background images
│   ├── red-carpet.jpg
│   ├── loungewear.jpg
│   ├── athleisurewear.jpg
│   ├── streetwear.jpg
│   └── proleisurewear.jpg
├── hero/               # Hero section background elements
│   └── hero-bg.jpg     (optional)
└── backgrounds/        # Additional background images
    └── (any additional backgrounds)
```

## Image Specifications

### Category Background Images

Place these images in `public/images/categories/`:

#### 1. **red-carpet.jpg**
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px minimum
- **Visual**: Dramatic red carpet, gala entrance, or fashion runway
- **Mood**: Glamorous, high-contrast, dramatic lighting
- **Action**: Model walking, fabric billowing, flash photography effects
- **Colors**: Deep blacks, rich crimsons, metallic accents

#### 2. **loungewear.jpg**
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px minimum
- **Visual**: Luxurious bedroom, serene home environment, morning light
- **Mood**: Soft, serene, intimate
- **Action**: Gentle movement, flowing fabric, relaxed poses
- **Colors**: Soft blush, sage, ivory, champagne, warm tones

#### 3. **athleisurewear.jpg**
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px minimum
- **Visual**: Urban fitness environment, gym, outdoor training space
- **Mood**: Dynamic, energetic, powerful
- **Action**: Running, yoga poses, athletic movement
- **Colors**: Bold energetic colors (coral, deep blue, mint, black)

#### 4. **streetwear.jpg**
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px minimum
- **Visual**: Urban environment, graffiti walls, city streets, subway stations
- **Mood**: Bold, authentic, edgy
- **Action**: Confident walking, urban poses, street style
- **Colors**: Bold contrasts, earth tones with neon accents, urban grays

#### 5. **proleisurewear.jpg**
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px minimum
- **Visual**: Modern home office, laptop workspace, bright home environment
- **Mood**: Professional yet comfortable, modern, clean
- **Action**: Working at desk, video call setup, transitioning between tasks
- **Colors**: Neutral professional tones (grays, blues, beiges) with coral accents

### Hero Section (Optional)
- **hero-bg.jpg** - Subtle background element for hero section
- Can be abstract or fashion-themed
- Should work well with gradient overlays

## Image Optimization Tips

1. **File Format**: Use .jpg for photographs, .png for graphics with transparency
2. **File Size**: Keep under 500KB per image (use compression tools)
3. **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px minimum for full-width backgrounds
4. **Quality**: High resolution but web-optimized
5. **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for desktop, consider 9:16 variants for mobile

## After Adding Images

Once you've placed your images in the correct folders, the website will automatically use them. The gradients will overlay on top of the images to ensure text readability while maintaining the visual impact of your photography.
